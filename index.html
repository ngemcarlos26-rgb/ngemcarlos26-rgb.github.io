The Breed

Two coats, one dog.

Chinese Cresteds come in two varieties from the same litter: Hairless, with soft skin and flowing furnishings on the head, feet, and tail, and Powderpuff, with a full double coat. Both are the same breed underneath — alert, affectionate, and sized for apartment life.
